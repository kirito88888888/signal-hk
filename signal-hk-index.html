The thesis

"AI, Web3, and finance aren't three separate industries anymore."

Hong Kong sits at the exact crossroads of three forces reshaping the global economy. No other city has the same density of crypto-native companies, a regulator actively building virtual asset frameworks, a deep bench of finance professionals, and a population that's tech-forward and capital-savvy.

Yet the people working across these domains rarely overlap. Signal HK exists to close that gap — a tight group of operators in the same city, same timezone, who meet in person and share what they're actually building.
